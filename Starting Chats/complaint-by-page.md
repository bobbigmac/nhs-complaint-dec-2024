## New Bank Health Centre, Manchester

### Page 1: Executive Summary
- Brief overview of key issues

### Page 2: Detailed Description of Issues
1. Digital-Only Access
   - No phone or in-person booking options
   - Impact on vulnerable groups
   - Accessibility concerns

2. Form Design and User Experience 
   - Small text fields
   - No save/resume functionality
   - Poor mobile experience

3. Communication Problems
   - Unscheduled callbacks
   - Lack of notifications
   - Request deletions without warning

4. Missed Call Policy
   - Two strikes deletion rule
   - Next-day resubmission requirement
   - Mischaracterization as "missed appointments"
