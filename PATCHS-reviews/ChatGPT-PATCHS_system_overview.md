- **“The vendor doesn’t support that.”** All major platforms support always-open intake with queueing, messaging, and direct booking; these are **configuration and policy** choices.
- **“Reception must use the system.”** Fine—**and** they must be able to book real slots. Proxying should help patients, not block them.
- **“Patients miss calls.”** Book exact times; if contact fails, send a link to reschedule—don’t wipe the record.
